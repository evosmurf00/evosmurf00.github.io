Welcome to Winning Smile

We are a small family friendly dental business.
We offer modern, innovative dentistry and competitive prices.
We are located in Cork. We have surgeries in Glanmire and Carraigaline.
Our practices are designed so that our patients feel as comfortable
and relaxed as possible and leave feeling happy with their dental experience.


We opened our doors in 1945 so you know we have alot of experience.
Our founder was Dr John Carter whom when setting up Winning smile wanted to change
the experience of going to the dentest.
He knew everyone hated going to the dentist so he wanted to change that.
